ABOUT US

Borouge najd is a leading Saudi Arabian plastic pipe manufacturing company and since our establishment we have constantly evolved to meet the requirements of our most demanding customers. Our aim is to provide sophisticated and diverse pipe solutions for hot and cold water applications, telecommunication networks, sewage and drainage systems together with water and gas infrastructure. We are committed to provide continuous innovation and investment in technology, in the relentless pursuit of providing quality products and services. Today, we are pleased to offer a wide range of plastic products, divided into four major categories categories, our factory is situated in Riyadh.
